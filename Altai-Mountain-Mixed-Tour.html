ALTAI MOUNTAIN MIXED TOUR

Altai Tavan Bogd Mountain area is one of the best destination in Western Mongolia. This tour is a combination of Altai Mountain trekking, Horse Riding adventure andStaying in a beautiful places of Altai Region.

TOUR ITINERARY

DAY-1: Arrival in Ulaanbaatar. We pick you up from Chinggis-Khaan Int’l Airport and transfer you to the hotel.

DAY-2: Flight to Ulgii and Drive to Ranger Station. Ulgii is a center of Bayan-Ulgii province located in the west of Mongolia. Flight from Ulaanbaatar to Ulgii lasts for 3 hours and our team pick you up from local airport. After having breakfast in restaurant, we start to drive to Ranger Station. Overnight at the ger camp (B+L+D)

DAY-3: Ride to Mount Altai TavanBogd. After breakfast we start our horse trekking towards Mount Altai Tavan Bogd and Potanin Glacier. Altai TavanBogd (means Holy Five Peaks) is the highest peaks in Mongolia. The Potanin Glacier lies at the foot of holy five peaks. We take a time to see this magnificent area and have lunch at the base camp. Ride back to the ranger station and stay overnight at the ger camp.(B+L+D)

DAY-4: Drive to Khoton Lake and Meet Local Eagle Hunter Family. We drive to the Khoton Lake which has a crystal clear water originating from Altai Mountains. The landscape around the Khoton Lake is very beautiful surrounded with snow-capped mountains. This is nice place for hiking, bird watching and photographing. We meet local nomad family to explore their life and spend night in a ger.(B+L+D)

DAY-5: Ride to Waterfall. In the morning, we drive to another extraordinary area “Ikh-Turgen Mountain” and go 2-3km trekking to see beautiful waterfall. We will have a time to feel the beauty of nature over there and also you may get one of your best shots during this trip. Late afternoon, we drive back and stay at same ger.(B+L+D)

DAY-6: Drive back to Ulgii.It will take approximately 5-6 hours to Ulgii. Overnight at tourist camp in Ulgii and we arrange Good-bye Party.(B+L+D)

DAY-7: Fly back to Ulaanbaatar. We drive you to the Ulgii airport. Flight will take 3 hours to Ulaanbaatar. Our driver will pick you up from airport and take you to the ChinggisKhaan statue located 54km away from Ulaanbaatar. Back to the hotel.(B+L+D)

DAY-8: Fly back to your country. Drive to the airport. End of the trip.(B+L+D)
